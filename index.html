Soil Search

Welcome to Soil Search. It is our mission to keep living phenonmenon safe. At Soil Search we use basic photos any user can take and see the plant's health. This will prevent plants from dying and causing danger to other people, such as trees falling on power lines or houses. Users may take photos of ceratin plants and we will see its species and see how much water is required for that particular plant in an easily understandible and receptive response.
